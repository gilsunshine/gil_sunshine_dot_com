Performance Machine

MIT Fall 2018
Course: 4.151 / Architecture Design Core Studio I
Instructors: Brandon Clifford, Jennifer Leung and Hans Tursack
TAs: Joseph Swerdlin, Mackenzie Muhonen and Michael Stradley


This project abstracts the landscape of the Emerald Necklace into an inhabitable performance machine. An aqueduct cuts through the length of park. At three sites along the length of the park there exists an architectural intervention that once a year is activated by a series of objects floated down the aqueduct. During the rest of the year, these sites offer paths through the park, performance spaces, places of gathering and reflection, and restrooms.
